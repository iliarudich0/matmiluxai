What is MiLux AI?

MatMiLux is a modern math-learning(and nnot only math) application designed for students who want to understand mathematics or other subjects step-by-step. The app contains theory, exercises, video explanations and a built-in AI assistant to guide you.

The app is currently in development and will be available on Google Play soon.
