Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

MatMiLux ("Application", "App") is developed by a private developer (“we”, “us”, “our”). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles user data.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

The MatMiLux app does not collect any personal data from users. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Device identifiers
  • Payment information

2. Information We Do Not Process

We do not sell, share, or transfer any data, because the App does not collect any user or device data.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party SDKs that collect personal information. Video tutorials may be loaded from a secure server, but no user tracking is performed.

4. Premium Features

The Premium button and Login features may be present in the interface, but they are disabled and non-functional. They do not collect nor process any user information.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is suitable for users of all ages and complies with Google Play’s Families policy. Because the App does not collect personal data, it does not knowingly collect information from children.

We may update this Privacy Policy if the functionality of the App changes. Updated versions will be posted on this page.
