TikLang AI Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 7, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to TikLang AI ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application TikLang AI (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you use our App, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Account Information: Email address, display name, and profile photo (if you sign in with Google)
  • User ID: A unique identifier assigned to your account
  • Authentication Data: Google account credentials when using Google Sign-In

1.2 Voice and Audio Data

  • Voice Recordings: We collect and process voice recordings when you use our AI conversation features
  • Audio Input: Real-time audio data for speech recognition and AI-powered language learning

1.3 Usage Data

  • Lesson Progress: Your learning history, completed lessons, and performance metrics
  • Usage Statistics: How often you use the app, which features you interact with, and session duration
  • Voice Usage Metrics: Duration of voice conversations for quota management

1.4 Subscription and Payment Information

  • Subscription Status: Whether you have an active Plus subscription
  • Purchase Tokens: Google Play billing tokens (we do NOT store credit card information)
  • Transaction History: Records of subscription purchases and renewals

1.5 Technical Data

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • Log Data: Error logs, crash reports, and diagnostic information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

2.1 Service Delivery

  • Provide AI-powered language learning and conversation practice
  • Process voice input and generate AI responses
  • Maintain your learning progress and personalized experience
  • Deliver premium features to Plus subscribers

2.2 Account Management

  • Authenticate and identify users
  • Manage subscriptions and billing
  • Enforce usage limits and quotas

2.3 Service Improvement

  • Analyze app usage to improve features and user experience
  • Debug technical issues and optimize performance
  • Monitor API usage and costs

2.4 Communication

  • Send important service notifications
  • Respond to user inquiries and support requests

3. Data Processing and Storage

3.1 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to process your data:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database storage, and cloud functions
  • OpenAI: AI-powered conversation and speech processing
  • Google Play Services: Subscription management and payment processing

3.2 Data Location

Your data is stored on Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) servers, which are located in various regions worldwide. Firebase complies with applicable data protection regulations including GDPR.

3.3 Data Retention

  • Voice Recordings: Temporary audio data is processed in real-time and NOT permanently stored on our servers
  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Usage Metrics: Retained for analytics purposes for up to 24 months
  • Subscription Data: Retained for billing and legal compliance purposes

4. Voice and AI Processing

4.1 Voice Data Handling

When you use voice features:

  • Audio is transmitted securely to OpenAI's API for processing
  • Voice data is used only for generating AI responses
  • We do NOT permanently store raw audio recordings
  • Transcripts and conversation logs may be retained for your learning history

4.2 OpenAI Data Processing

OpenAI processes your voice input according to their privacy policy. We recommend reviewing OpenAI's privacy policy at: https://openai.com/privacy

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal data. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

5.1 Service Providers

  • OpenAI: For AI conversation processing
  • Google/Firebase: For hosting, authentication, and cloud services
  • Google Play: For subscription verification

5.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request.

5.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication via Firebase Authentication
  • Access controls and authentication requirements for API endpoints
  • Regular security audits and updates

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

7.1 Access and Portability

You can request a copy of your personal data.

7.2 Correction

You can update or correct your account information.

7.3 Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

7.4 Withdrawal of Consent

You can withdraw consent for data processing by deleting your account.

7.5 Opt-Out

You can opt out of optional data collection by not using certain features.

8. Children's Privacy

TikLang AI is NOT intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. International Users

If you are accessing our App from outside the European Economic Area (EEA), please note that your data may be transferred to and processed in countries with different data protection laws.

10. Subscription and Billing

10.1 Free Plan

  • Limited to 60 seconds of lifetime voice conversation
  • No payment information collected

10.2 Plus Subscription

  • Managed through Google Play subscriptions
  • Billing handled by Google (we do NOT store credit card details)

10.3 Refunds

Refund requests are handled according to Google Play's refund policy.
