AI House Redesign Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 7, 2026

Last Updated: February 7, 2026

Welcome to AI House Redesign ("we," "us," or "our"), an AI-powered app that allows users to redesign and visualize home interiors and exteriors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: When you sign in via Google, we collect your email address, display name, and profile picture from Google Sign-In.
  • User-Generated Content: Photos, masks, prompts, and redesign outputs you upload or generate within the App.

1.2 Usage Data

  • App Usage: Information about how you interact with the App, including features used, time spent, and device information (e.g., device type, operating system, app version).
  • Job Metadata: Details of your redesign jobs, such as tool selected, presets chosen, timestamps, and job status.
  • Analytics and Crash Reports: We use Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics to collect anonymous usage statistics and error reports to improve the App.

1.3 Technical Data

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, and location data (if enabled).
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We may use cookies or similar technologies for authentication and analytics.

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as financial data, health information, or precise location data unless explicitly provided by you.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the App's AI redesign features.
  • To authenticate your account and manage your subscription.
  • To process and store your photos, masks, and generated redesigns.
  • To improve the App's performance, functionality, and user experience.
  • To communicate with you about updates, support, or promotional offers (with your consent).
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except as described below:

  • Service Providers: We use third-party services to operate the App:
    • Google Firebase: For authentication, data storage, analytics, and crash reporting.
    • OpenAI: To process images and generate AI redesigns using their API. Images are sent securely and temporarily for processing.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, to protect our rights, or in response to a legal request.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes encryption of data in transit and at rest, secure server environments, and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained as long as your account is active.
  • User-Generated Content: Stored in your account until you delete it or request deletion.
  • Usage Data: Retained for up to 2 years for analytics and improvement purposes.

We may retain data longer if required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

6. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed and stored in servers located outside your country, including in the United States. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information promptly.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access and Portability: Request a copy of your data.
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Opt-Out: Withdraw consent for marketing communications or disable analytics tracking in your device settings.
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data.

To exercise these rights, contact us at matmiluxai@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the App or via email. Your continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
